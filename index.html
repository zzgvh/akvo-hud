What Is Akvo Under The Hood?

Akvo is a non-profit foundation headquartered in the Netherlands, with colleagues worldwide. We help connect doers and donors to transform communities in some of the poorest parts of the world.

We use and develop open source software. All of our code is available on Github. But this isn't enough. Open source software needs a community, to innovate, offer support and to DO stuff. Be the bleeding edge, helping us innovate and push boundaries. And this is where you come in.
